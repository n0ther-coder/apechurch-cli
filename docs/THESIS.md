# Why Ape Church? The Permissionless Advantage

Traditional online casinos are walled gardens. To build a bot, you'd need:
- API keys (if they even offer one)
- Account approval
- Terms of service that ban automation
- Rate limits, IP restrictions, KYC
- The casino's permission to play

**Ape Church is different.** It's fully on-chain, fully decentralized.

---

## No Keys. No Accounts. No Permission.

The Ape Church CLI doesn't use any API. There's no backend to authenticate with, no server to ask permission from. Every game is a smart contract on ApeChain. Every bet is a blockchain transaction.

**All you need is:**
1. A wallet (the CLI generates one for you)
2. APE to play with

That's it. No signup. No approval. No API key. No rate limits.

---

## Why This Matters

- **Bots are first-class citizens** — The contracts don't know or care if you're human or AI
- **No gatekeepers** — Nobody can revoke your access or ban your account
- **Provably fair** — VRF on-chain means verifiable randomness, not "trust us"
- **Composable** — Anyone can build on top of the same contracts
- **Unstoppable** — As long as ApeChain runs, the games run

---

## The Bottom Line

Centralized casinos tolerate players. Ape Church welcomes builders.

You can't build a trading bot for a casino that doesn't want bots. But Ape Church is just smart contracts — it doesn't have opinions. It doesn't have a legal team. It just executes code.

**This is what permissionless gaming looks like.**
